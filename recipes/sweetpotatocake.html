Looking for a sweet that you wont regret? look no further. These sweet potato cakes are full of fiber and nutrients and make dessert a little less daunting for the health concious

  • 1 Large Baked Sweet Potato (oven roasted and cooled ahead of time)
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 Cup Lowfat Milk
  • 1/2 cup of Honey or Maple Syrup
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 Tablespoon Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon (or several shakes) of Nutmeg or other warming spices
  • (optional) 1 scoop of protein powder
  • (optional) 1 tablespoon Vanilla bean paste
