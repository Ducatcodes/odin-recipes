Green Sauce

This Green sauce recipe is so good you will almost forget how packed it is with nutrients, fiber, and protein

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Bag of Spinach
  • 1 Large Bag of Kale
  • 2-3 Handfulls of cashews
  • 1-2 cups of parmasean cheese to taste
  • Chicken or bone broth as needed
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • (optional) Cream
  • (optional) red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Wash and dry the spinach and kale thoroughly. Remove any tough stems from the kale.
  2. Blanch the Greens: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spinach and kale and cook for 1-2 minutes until bright green and softened.
  3. Blend the Sauce: In a blender or food processor, combine the blanched spinach, kale, raw cashews, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and a splash of chicken or bone broth. Blend until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed. Add more broth, little by little, until the sauce reaches your desired consistency.
  4. Season:Add salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes to taste. Blend briefly to combine.
  5. Add Cream (Optional):If using cream, drizzle it into the blender and pulse a few times until incorporated for a richer sauce.
  6. Taste and Adjust:Taste the sauce and adjust seasoning as needed. Add more Parmesan for nuttiness, broth for thinning, or garlic for more kick.
  7. Serve:Use immediately as a pasta sauce, topping for meats and vegetables, or as a dip.
  8. Store:Transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 3-4 days. For longer storage, freeze the sauce in small portions.
  • Tips for Best Results
  • If the sauce feels too thick, add more broth in small increments to smooth it out.
  • A squeeze of lemon juice can add a fresh note if desired.
