The Healthy Meatball

Level Up your mealprep with these Meatballs. Adding grated vegtables to the mix not only makes them extra delicious (although a bit unorthodox)but gives them a serious punch of vitamins and fiber to keep you fueled

  • 2 LB lean ground beef
  • 1 LB ground pork
  • 1 Cup Breadcumbs
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups(ish) Finely grated Carrots
  • 2 Cups(ish) Finely grated Zuchinni
  • 2 Cups(ish) Finely grated Onion
  • 2 Tablespoons(ish) of both salt and pepper
  • 1 Cup Parsley chopped
